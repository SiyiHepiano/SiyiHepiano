Siyi He

Siyi He, studying Master’s Degree in Royal academy of music, is a brilliant piano performer and piano accompanist artist. She had the privilege of being an opera coach for the opera Gianni Schicchi composed by Puccini. As a successful performer and artist, she obtained high comments and ranking in the competitions and artistic activities, which includes as a Jury of the Liszt International Piano Competition and National Wanda Cup. Hailun Piano Competition Guangxi Outstanding Instructor Award

Biography

  • Siyi He, studying Master’s Degree in Royal academy of music, is a brilliant piano performer and piano accompanist artist. Born in 1996, she studied piano since she was 5 years old and then studied performance from Professor Congcong Chai at the Wuhan Conservatory of music. Meanwhile, she also concentrated on Vocal performance as well since age of 19. She was admitted to the Piano department in Wuhan Conservatory of music as a bachelor student in 2014.
  • In the duration of Wuhan Conservatory of music, she achieved highly praise for her performance from her classmates and faculty members including private professor, and proactively attended in different types of performing activities. She also cooperated with other young artists in school, performing vocal and instrumental repertoires as accompanist in significant activities and examinations.
  • As a successful performer and artist, she obtained high comments and ranking in the competitions and artistic activities, which includes as a Jury of the Liszt International Piano Competition and National Wanda Cup. Hailun Piano Competition Guangxi Outstanding Instructor Award
